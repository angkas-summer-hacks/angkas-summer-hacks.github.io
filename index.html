View Mechanics

Join us for an exhilarating 24-hour hackathon where creativity and innovation take center stage! This event is open to all departments, with a special encouragement for non-tech participants. Get ready to collaborate, code, and compete!

Mechanics

  • Participating teams must register to enter and are exempt from work.
  • Maximum of 3 participants per team.
  • Bonus points for applying the given keyword/topic.
  • No frivolous presentations; a functional application that judges can interact with is required.
  • 24-hour time limit.
  • Participants must prepare a 5-minute presentation, followed by a Q&A session.

  • Day 1 8:00am breakfast

  • Day 1 9:00am start
  • Day 1 12:00pm lunch
  • Day 1 7:00pm dinner
  • Day 2 12:00am snacks
  • Day 2 7:00am wake-up call
  • Day 2 8:00am finalize output
  • Day 2 9:00am submission
  • Day 2 11:00am brunch
  • Day 2 2:00pm-4:00pm presentation and awarding

Criteria

Category Breakdown

  • Completeness (40%): Assurance that your project does everything it's supposed to do without any game breaking problems.
  • Creativity (30%): Come up with a unique idea and present it in an interesting way.
  • Innovation (20%): Try something new and different that could really shake things up.
  • Business Impact (10%): How your project could actually be used in the real world to make money or solve problems.

Bonus Points

  • Topic Relevancy (up to 10 points): Keep your project focused on the main theme of the hackathon.
  • bB2B Implementation (up to 5 points): If your project could fit well into how businesses work together.
