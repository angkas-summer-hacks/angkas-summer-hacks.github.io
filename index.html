View Mechanics

Join us for an exhilarating 24-hour hackathon where creativity and innovation take center stage! This event is open to all departments, with a special encouragement for non-tech participants. Get ready to collaborate, code, and compete!

Mechanics

  • Duration: 24 hours, from 9 AM on May 23rd to 9 AM on May 24th
  • Topic: Will be revealed at the start of the event
  • Amenities: Enjoy free snacks throughout the event
  • Eligibility: Open to all departments, but requires approval from your line manager (Tech and Product departments are pre-approved)
  • Team Size: Maximum of 3 participants per team. Deadline for registration ends one day before the event
  • Overnight Stay: Allowed in the office for the duration of the hackathon
  • No bullsh*t presentation, we require a playable output that judges can interact with it.
  • Prepare a 5-minute presentation, with Q & A

Event Schedule

START (Thursday)

  • 8:00 - 8:30 AM: Assembly with snacks and coffee
  • 9:30 - 9:00 AM: Opening remarks
  • 9:00 AM: Gun start and topic reveal
  • 12:00 - 1:00 PM: Snack break

FINAL (Friday)

  • 12:00 - 1:00 AM: Snack break
  • 7:00 AM: Wake-up call to finalize work
  • 8:00 AM: Last call for cut-off submissions
  • 9:00 AM: Output cutoff
  • 2:00 - 4:00 PM: Presentation, judging, and awarding ceremony

Judging Criteria

10-point system. Each category can score max to 10 as highest. Points can go up to 100pts base on their respective percentage.

Breakdown of % per category:

  • completeness: 40%
  • cool/fun/engaging: 30%
  • innovative: 25%
  • topic relevancy: 5%

Bonus Points:

  • business impact: max 10pts
  • b2b implementation: max 5pts

Notes

  • Officially registered team and participants are exempted from work. Make sure to get approval from your line manager first (unless you're from Tech or Product).
  • Bring your best ideas and prepare for an overnight coding marathon.
  • We encourage participants from non-tech departments to join and bring a fresh perspective to the hackathon.
